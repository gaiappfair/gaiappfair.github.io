GAI APP FAIR 2015

February 27, 6:00 PM - GSU Backcourt

What is the App Fair?

Global App Initiative members have been working
hard to design and program mobile apps for
non-profit organizations, and this will be a chance for
them to show off their apps.

Who can attend?

This event is open to anyone who would like to
check out all of the awesome GAI apps. We
encourage all GAI team members to attend this event
to get valuable feedback and inspiration from other apps.
